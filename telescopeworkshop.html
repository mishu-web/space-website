Stargazing Telescope Workshops

About Our Workshop

At SpaceXplore, we bring the wonders of the universe closer to you through our hands-on telescope workshops. Founded in 2015 by a team of astronomy enthusiasts, our local company has been helping people of all ages explore the night sky with professional-grade equipment.

Our workshops are designed for both beginners and intermediate astronomy lovers. You'll learn how to operate different types of telescopes, understand celestial coordinates, and observe planets, stars, and deep-sky objects.

Workshop Details

Our signature 3-hour workshop includes:

  • Introduction to telescope types and their operation
  • Hands-on practice with our premium telescopes
  • Night sky navigation techniques
  • Observation of visible planets and celestial objects
  • Astrophotography basics (for advanced workshops)
  • Q&A session with our astronomy experts

All equipment is provided, though you're welcome to bring your own telescope for personalized guidance.

Upcoming Schedule

Workshops are held every Friday and Saturday evening, weather permitting. Special lunar observation events are scheduled during full moon phases.

Each session is limited to 15 participants to ensure personalized attention.

What You'll See

Depending on the season and weather conditions, you may observe:

  • Jupiter and its Galilean moons
  • Saturn's magnificent rings
  • Lunar craters and maria
  • Mars (during opposition)
  • Bright star clusters like the Pleiades
  • Andromeda Galaxy (on clear nights)
