Exploring the Cosmos Together

At SpaceXplore, we're passionate about making astronomy accessible to everyone and fostering a community of space enthusiasts.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of astronomy enthusiasts, SpaceXplore began as a small community of stargazers meeting in local parks. What started as informal telescope viewing sessions has grown into a thriving space education center with thousands of members across the country.

Our founders recognized that many people were fascinated by space but lacked access to equipment and knowledgeable guides. SpaceXplore was created to bridge this gap, providing high-quality telescopes and expert-led experiences to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Mission

To democratize access to astronomical education and equipment, inspiring wonder and scientific curiosity through direct experiences with the cosmos.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has the opportunity to explore the universe firsthand, fostering a deeper connection with science and our place in the cosmos.

Our Facilities

Our headquarters in Cosmos City features:

  • A state-of-the-art observatory with a 16-inch research-grade telescope
  • Classroom spaces for workshops and lectures
  • An astronomy library with hundreds of volumes
  • Outdoor viewing platforms with portable telescopes
  • Virtual reality space exploration station

We also operate mobile telescope units that visit schools and community centers throughout the region.
